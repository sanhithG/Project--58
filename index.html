How I got to like tennis.

My favorite hobby is playing tennis. I started playing tennis when I was 4 years old. I only started like tennis at age 7. Tennis keeps you fit and is good exercise. Tennis is fun and a good game that you can play with family and friends! Tennis is only a 2 player or 4 player game. Tennis is very dangerous to play in wet conditions, so please do not try. If you are going to play tennis a lot it can make you thirsty. So please bring water to keep you hydrated. Tennis is a fun and exciting game that I like to play a lot!
